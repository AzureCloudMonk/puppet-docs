# The Puppet Forge

The [Puppet Forge](https://forge.puppet.com/) is a repository of modules for Puppet written by both Puppet and the Puppet community. These modules can help you extend Puppet across your infrastructure and help you get your work done faster.

### Choosing and installing modules

* [Learn about modules.](https://docs.puppet.com/puppet/latest/modules_fundamentals.html)
* [Installing modules.](https://docs.puppet.com/puppet/latest/modules_installing.html)
* [Assess the quality of a module on the Puppet Forge.](https://docs.puppet.com/forge/assessingmodulequality.md)
